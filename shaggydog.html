About Us

She has a shih Tzu named Pooki, an elderly miniature pinscher named Sparky that she and her husband found on the streets and rehabbed, two rescued pit mixes named Luna and Sully, two cats named Yoshi and Kimbo, and the sweetest off track thoroughbred horse named Leo! Her clients at Shaggy Dog become an extension of her family, and she loves each and every one of her pet clients as if they were her very own!
